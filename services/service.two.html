Blooming Celebrations

At The Beauty Shop, we offer a wide range of fresh and beautiful flowers that are perfect for cakes and decorations. Our expert florists can help you choose the perfect flowers to complement your cake or create stunning floral arrangements that will add a special touch to your event.

For cakes, we offer custom flower toppers that are made from fresh flowers and greenery. Our florists can work with you to select the right colors and flowers to match the cake, creating a unique and personalized topper that will make your cake even more special.

We also offer a range of floral arrangements that can be used to decorate your event space. From table centerpieces to room decorations, our florists can create stunning designs that will add an elegant and sophisticated touch to your event. We offer a variety of flowers, from classic roses and lilies to more exotic blooms like orchids and protea.

At The Beauty Shop, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest and highest-quality flowers in all of our designs. Our expert florists are dedicated to creating beautiful and unique designs that are tailored to your needs and preferences. Contact us today to learn more about our flower options for cakes and decorations, and to start planning your perfect event.
