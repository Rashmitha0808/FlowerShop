Welcome to The beauty house

A family-owned and operated business dedicated to providing our customers with the most beautiful and fresh flowers. Our shop was founded by Rashmitha in 2023, and since then, we have been committed to providing top-notch customer service and stunning floral designs.
At The beauty house, we believe that flowers have the power to brighten up any room and bring joy to people's lives. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, sending a gift to a loved one, or just looking to add some color to your home, we have a wide range of flowers and arrangements to suit every need.

At The beauty house, we believe that every customer deserves personalized and attentive service. That's why we take the time to understand our customers' needs and preferences, and work closely with them to create the perfect design. We also offer delivery services to make it easy for our customers to surprise their loved ones with a gorgeous bouquet. Thank you for choosing The beauty house. We look forward to working with you to create a stunning floral arrangement that will bring a smile to your face!
